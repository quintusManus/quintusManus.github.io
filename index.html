EcoImpact

EcoImpact is an interactive web application that helps users explore humanity’s environmental impact across Air, Water, and Ground. With data-driven visualizations and hands-on simulations, EcoImpact transforms climate metrics into compelling, intuitive experiences. Features include carbon footprint comparisons, interactive emissions maps, ocean plastic projections, and engaging simulations of material degradation and farm emissions. Built with FastAPI, PostgreSQL, and a React/D3.js frontend, EcoImpact empowers users to understand and reduce their environmental footprint.

EchoLearn (HackUNCP2025)

EchoLearn is a language learning web app built for HackUNCP2025, designed to help users improve their language skills through interactive lessons and quizzes. Powered by Flask and the OpenAI API, EchoLearn delivers personalized grammar and vocabulary modules, preliminary assessments, and adaptive lesson plans. The app provides a structured learning path, tracks user progress, and leverages AI to create a dynamic, engaging educational experience.
